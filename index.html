ANubela Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-xx-xx

ANubela (“the App”, “we”) is a personal journaling experience designed by Cosmic Diary Studio. We take privacy seriously and built the product so that your entries stay on your device. This policy describes what data we collect, how it is used, and what control you have.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information on our servers. All information stays locally on your iOS device by default.

1.1 Data You Enter

Mood selections / stickers / Soul Question
Stored locally using Apple’s SwiftData framework. The data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through iOS system features (e.g., iCloud backup) or export screenshots.

1.2 Device Information

The App only uses Apple system APIs necessary to render the interface and store data locally. We do not access contacts, location, calendars, or motion sensors.

1.3 Photos Permission

When you choose to export a generated sticker to your Photos library, the App asks for photo permissions. The images are written directly to your library; no copy is uploaded to us.

2. Data Sharing

We do not run servers, analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party data processors. Therefore, no personal data is shared with anyone.

3. On-Device Storage & Security

  • All entries are stored using SwiftData on your device’s encrypted storage.
  • If your device has a passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID enabled, Apple’s system-level encryption protects the data at rest.
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored information.

4. Children’s Privacy

ANubela is suitable for users aged 4+ and does not contain content targeted specifically at children. We intentionally avoid collecting any personal information.

5. Your Choices

  • Export: You may export stickers or screenshots using iOS sharing options.
  • Delete Data: Delete the App from your device to remove all stored information.
  • Photos Access: Revoke photo permissions anytime via iOS Settings > ANubela.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce cloud sync, analytics, or other online services, we will update this policy and highlight the changes inside the App and on our website before they take effect.

7. Contact

If you have questions or requests about privacy, email support@example.com. We will respond within 7 business days.
